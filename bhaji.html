Traditional Sindhi Bhaji Recipes

Basar ji Bughal Bhaji (Onion Stir Fry)

Ingredients:
  • 3 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Sprinkle turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Cook until soft and fragrant.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with phulkas.

Besan ji Aani (Gram Flour Curry)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
Steps:
  1. Roast besan lightly in a dry pan until aromatic.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin and green chilies.
  3. Mix roasted besan with water, add spices, and stir well.
  4. Simmer until thick and smooth.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Besan ji Bhaji (Gupta)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and onions.
  2. Stir in tomatoes and spices, cook until soft.
  3. Add besan and mix quickly.
  4. Cook until it thickens into a soft curry.
  5. Serve with rotis or rice.

Daal Turi (Tur Dal Bhaji)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup tur dal (pigeon pea)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green chili
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Boil tur dal until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, onion, and chili.
  3. Stir in tomatoes and spices, cook till soft.
  4. Add boiled dal and simmer.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.

Methi Paata (Fenugreek Leaves Bhaji)

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups fresh methi leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and onion.
  2. Stir in methi leaves, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  3. Cook until the leaves are soft and oil separates.
  4. Serve with rotis or steamed rice.

Paalak Patata (Spinach with Potatoes)

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Heat oil, fry onion until golden.
  2. Add potatoes, cook till half done.
  3. Add spinach, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Cook until potatoes are tender and spinach soft.
  5. Serve with hot phulkas.

Daal Paalak (Dal with Spinach)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup moong dal or tur dal
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Boil dal until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin and green chili.
  3. Stir in spinach and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add dal, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes and serve hot.

Seyal Mani (Phulke ji Bhaji)

Ingredients:
  • 4 phulkas (roti), torn into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Heat oil, add cumin and onions, fry until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook till pulpy.
  3. Add torn phulkas and mix well.
  4. Cook until soft and flavors are absorbed.
  5. Serve warm as a comfort meal.
