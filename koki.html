Traditional Sindhi Recipes

Basar Paati Koki

Basar ji koki youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Mix wheat flour, onion, green chilies, coriander, cumin, ajwain, salt, and oil.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a firm dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Roll into thick flatbreads.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with a little ghee until golden and crisp on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.

Besani (Besan ji Koki)

besan ji koki youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Mix wheat flour, gram flour, onion, chilies, coriander, cumin, turmeric, salt, and oil.
  2. Knead into a stiff dough using water gradually.
  3. Roll thick discs.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve with pickle or curd.

Kare Mirch Wari Koki

Kare mirch wari koki
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3-4 green chilies, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Mix wheat flour, green chilies, black pepper, cumin, coriander, salt, and oil.
  2. Knead into a firm dough with water.
  3. Rest for 10 minutes, then roll thick rotis.
  4. Roast on tawa with ghee until crisp and golden.
  5. Serve spicy koki with yogurt or papad.

Sindhi Kadhi

Sindhi kadhi youtube link
Ingredients:
  • ½ cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (drumsticks, ladyfinger, beans, carrots, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 cups water
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin, fenugreek, and red chilies.
  2. Add besan and roast till golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and water. Stir well.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook until soft.
  5. Stir in tamarind pulp and simmer for 10 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Besan jo Chilo

Besan jo chilo youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking
Steps:
  1. Mix besan with onion, chilies, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Add water to make a smooth batter.
  3. Heat a tawa, pour a ladle of batter, and spread like a pancake.
  4. Cook both sides with little oil until golden.
  5. Serve hot with green chutney or curd.
