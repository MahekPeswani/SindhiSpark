Traditional Sindhi Rice Recipes

Bughal Chawar

bughal chawar youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 black cardamoms
  • 4 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil/ghee, add whole spices and sauté sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, green chilies, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until masala thickens.
  4. Add soaked rice and water (about 4 cups). Mix gently.
  5. Cover and cook until rice is fluffy and aromatic.
  6. Serve hot with raita or papad.

Tayari Pila Chawar

tayari chawar youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 black cardamom
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
Steps:
  1. Soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, bay leaf, cardamom, and cloves.
  3. Add onions and fry until golden.
  4. Mix in turmeric and salt.
  5. Add soaked rice and water (about 4 cups).
  6. Cook covered till rice is done and yellow in color.
  7. Serve with Sindhi kadhi or fried papad.

Sai Daal Paata Chawar

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup split green moong dal (with skin)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Soak dal and rice separately for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, ginger, and green chilies.
  3. Add drained dal, sauté for 2 minutes, then add rice.
  4. Pour in 4 cups water and add salt.
  5. Cook covered until both dal and rice are soft and fluffy.
  6. Serve with curd, pickle, or papad.

Chana Daal Paata Chawar

chana daal pata chawar youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup chana dal
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 2 green chilies
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Soak chana dal for 1 hour. Wash and soak rice separately.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, onion, and green chilies. Sauté until onions are golden.
  3. Add turmeric, salt, soaked dal, and sauté for 5 minutes.
  4. Add rice and 4 cups water. Mix gently.
  5. Cook until rice and dal are done but separate.
  6. Serve with papad and pickle.

Aur Wara Chawar

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee, add whole spices and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add soaked rice and salt. Stir gently.
  4. Add water (about 4 cups) and cook covered until done.
  5. Serve with Sindhi curry or dal.

Jeere Paata Chawar

jeera pata chawar youtube link
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
Steps:
  1. Soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add soaked rice and salt. Mix lightly.
  4. Add water (about 4 cups) and cook till fluffy.
  5. Serve with Sindhi kadhi or dal.

Masala Paata Chawar

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Heat oil, fry onions till golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook till masala thickens.
  3. Add rice and 4 cups water.
  4. Cover and cook till rice is fluffy and masala-coated.
  5. Serve with papad or raita.

Methi Paata Chawar

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup fresh methi leaves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Steps:
  1. Wash rice and soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions, and green chilies.
  3. Add methi leaves, turmeric, and sauté for 3 minutes.
  4. Add rice, salt, and 4 cups water.
  5. Cook until rice is fluffy and aromatic.
  6. Serve with kadhi or curd.

Dahi Chawar

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup fresh curd
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
Steps:
  1. Whisk curd with salt and roasted cumin.
  2. Mix in cooked rice, green chili, and coriander.
  3. Serve chilled with papad or pickle.
