About SindhiSpark

Who We Are

Every culture carries stories that deserve to be remembered. SindhiSpark began with a simple belief: that Sindhiyat should not only be preserved, but celebrated, shared, and passed on to future generations.

SindhiSpark is a youth-led initiative dedicated to bringing together the richness of Sindhi literature, folk stories, traditions, recipes, festivals, music, and the inspiring journeys of Sindhi youth. It is our small effort to create a space where heritage is not only remembered, but experienced, appreciated, and shared with the world.

Our Mission

We hope to make Sindhi heritage easier to discover, more engaging to explore, and something younger generations are proud to carry forward. Through stories, traditions, recipes, literature, and youth initiatives, SindhiSpark aims to inspire curiosity, strengthen cultural identity, and build meaningful connections across generations.

Because culture stays alive when people choose to share it.

The Story Behind SindhiSpark

We are Mahek and Kabir Peswani, two young students who believe that even a small idea can help keep a culture alive. As we learned more about our family's roots in Gadi Mori, part of the former Khairpur State, and listened to the stories our grandparents shared about the role our ancestors played within the community, we found ourselves becoming increasingly curious about the history, traditions, and heritage of Sindh.

At the same time, we realised that many young people were gradually becoming disconnected from their roots. That inspired us to create a place where learning about Sindhiyat could feel meaningful, accessible, and enjoyable.

What began as an idea has grown into SindhiSpark—our humble tribute to our ancestors, our language, and our community. We hope this platform encourages more young people to take pride in their roots while inspiring others to contribute their own stories, memories, and experiences.

Why SindhiSpark?

Every culture is built on stories. Sindhiyat lives in the poems passed down through generations, the recipes prepared with love, the traditions celebrated by families, the music that brings people together, and the dreams of young people who continue to carry their heritage forward.

SindhiSpark exists to celebrate these stories, create connections across generations, and ensure that the spirit of Sindhiyat continues to inspire people for years to come.
