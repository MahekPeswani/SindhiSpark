Traditional Sindhi Kofta Recipes

Mix Bhajee ja Kofta (Mixed Vegetable Kofta)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato), boiled & mashed
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mash all vegetables and potatoes together.
  2. Add besan, spices, green chili, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Shape into small balls.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve with curry or chutney.

Suran ja Kofta

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups suran, boiled and mashed
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mash boiled suran and mix with besan, spices, chili, and ginger paste.
  2. Shape into koftas (balls).
  3. Deep fry until crisp and golden.
  4. Serve with Sindhi curry or dal.

Kaddu ja Kofta (Bottle Gourd Kofta)

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups grated kaddu (bottle gourd), squeezed
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix grated kaddu with besan, onion, spices, and salt.
  2. Form small koftas from the mixture.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Enjoy with curry, rice, or rotis.

Gobi ja Kofta (Cauliflower Kofta)

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups grated gobi (cauliflower)
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix gobi, potatoes, besan, spices, and chili together.
  2. Shape into round koftas.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil until golden.
  4. Serve with curry or chutney.
