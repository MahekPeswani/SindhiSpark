Traditional Sindhi Pakoda Recipes

Sindhi Pakoda

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
Steps:
  1. Mix besan with onion, chilies, ajwain, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to make a thick batter.
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai, drop spoonfuls of batter.
  4. Fry until golden and crisp.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Patatan ja Pakoda (Potato Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 2 large potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix besan with turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, salt, and water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Dip potato slices in the batter.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil till golden and crisp.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Basar ja Pakoda (Onion Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix onions with green chilies, besan, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Sprinkle little water to bind the mixture.
  3. Heat oil and drop small portions into it.
  4. Fry till crisp and golden.
  5. Serve hot with tea or chutney.

Paalak ja Pakoda (Spinach Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 10 spinach leaves, washed and dried
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Prepare a smooth batter with besan, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, salt, and water.
  2. Dip spinach leaves in the batter.
  3. Fry in hot oil until crisp.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.

Bhee ja Pakoda (Lotus Stem Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup lotus stem slices (bhee), boiled
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix besan, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, salt with water to form a batter.
  2. Dip boiled bhee slices in the batter.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve with green chutney.

Mirch ja Pakoda (Chili Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 6 large green chilies
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Slit chilies lengthwise, remove seeds if preferred.
  2. Make a batter with besan, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, salt, and water.
  3. Dip chilies in batter and deep fry till golden.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.

Ambadi ja Pakoda (Gongura/Sorrel Leaves Pakoda)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup ambadi (gongura/sorrel) leaves, chopped
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp ajwain
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Mix ambadi leaves, onion, chilies with besan, turmeric, chili powder, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Add little water to form a thick mixture.
  3. Drop spoonfuls in hot oil and fry till crisp.
  4. Serve tangy pakodas hot with chutney.
